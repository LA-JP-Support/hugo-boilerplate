## What is BotStar?

**BotStar is a platform that allows you to build and manage AI-powered chatbots across multiple channels (websites, Facebook, WhatsApp, LINE, etc.) without programming knowledge. Using a drag-and-drop visual editor, you design conversation flows and instantly deploy them across multiple channels. It handles customer support automation, lead generation, appointment booking, and much more.**

> **In a nutshell:** "Build one chatbot, and it automatically deploys to Facebook, WhatsApp, and your website"—a smart system.

**Key points:**

- **What it does:** Build and manage AI chatbots with no-code platform
- **Why it matters:** Reduces development costs, enabling more companies to achieve automation
- **Who uses it:** Marketing departments, customer service teams, IT departments

## How it works

BotStar uses a 3-step process. First, design your bot's conversation flow using the visual editor. For example: "Hello" → "How can I help?" → "Order." Next, configure [natural language processing](NLP.md) to recognize user intent. Finally, connect to channels like Facebook and WhatsApp, and the bot automatically deploys across all of them.

An [analytics dashboard](Analytics.md) lets you track inquiries by channel and completion rates for each question.

## Frequently asked questions

**Q: How do I get started with BotStar?**
A: After creating an account, select a template or design your conversation flow from scratch. Just follow the visual editor's guidance.

**Q: When deploying to multiple channels, don't questions duplicate?**
A: BotStar integrates channels unified management, ensuring consistent customer experience. Different flows per channel are also possible.

**Q: Can I improve after deployment?**
A: Yes. Identify improvements from analytics data and instantly modify and redeploy through the visual editor.

## Competing and alternative services

Similar platforms include [Botpress](Botpress.md), Chatfuel, and Dialogflow. BotStar's strengths are especially in no-code design and multi-language support.
