## What is Rich Media Cards?

**Rich Media Cards are interactive UI components chatbots use to combine images, buttons, and text.** More visually compelling than plain text, users see images, click buttons, and take action immediately. Like a mini web page embedded in chat.

> **In a nutshell:** Chatbot evolution from "text-only" to "attractive image + description + buttons."

**Key points:**

- **What it does:** Displays combined text, image, and button messages in card format
- **Why it's needed:** Visual clarity improves understanding; user actions increase
- **Who uses it:** Ecommerce, customer support, lead generation companies

## Why it matters

Text-only chatbots cause scroll fatigue and abandonment. Rich Media Cards show info at a glance; clear next actions like "Buy Now" or "Learn More" drive conversion. Visual information sticks in memory; user satisfaction rises. Cards work across Facebook Messenger, WhatsApp, and other platforms, enabling cross-channel consistency.

## How it works

Rich Media Cards consist of three elements:

**Element 1: Visual.** Product images, GIFs, videos. First thing users see. Ideally under 100KB.

**Element 2: Text.** Title (product name) and subtitle (description, price). Concise and informative.

**Element 3: Action.** Buttons like "Buy Now" or "See Details." User next steps. Suggested reply chips (chatbot-offered options) are included.

For Ecommerce recommendation: "Product image → title + price → 'Buy Now' button" flows naturally.

## Real-world use cases

**Ecommerce product recommendations**

Bot asks "Which category?" User selects. Bot shows multiple products with images. Each has "Buy Now." Purchase rates 3-5x higher than text-only.

**Customer support troubleshooting**

"What's wrong?" "Solution A" "Solution B" progressively guides. Final "Resolved?" YES/NO buttons capture satisfaction in real-time.

**Lead capture forms**

Chat-embedded forms (name, email, phone) display as cards. Form format beats text requests; collective input more efficient.

## Benefits and considerations

Rich Media Cards' greatest benefit is engagement and conversion improvement. Visual clarity means 3-5x higher click rates than text.

Limitations: Platform support differs. Facebook Messenger and WhatsApp support different card types; multi-platform requires adjustments. Media file size management matters.

## Frequently asked questions

**Q: Do all chat platforms support Rich Media Cards?**

A: No. Facebook Messenger has extensive support; WhatsApp is limited. Multi-platform adaptation is needed.

**Q: What button actions are possible?**

A: "Website link," "phone call," "email send," "app open," and more. Platform specs limit options; check chatbot provider docs.

**Q: What's ideal image size?**

A: Under 100KB recommended. High-res delays load, damaging experience. 16:9 or 3:2 aspect ratios suit mobile displays.
