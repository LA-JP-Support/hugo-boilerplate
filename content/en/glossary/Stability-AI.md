## Primary Products and Services

**Stable Diffusion** - A diffusion model generating high-quality images from text prompts. Published open-source, widely used from individuals to enterprises.

**Stable Video Diffusion** - Technology generating video from still images or text. Enables efficient video production and prototyping.

**Stable Code** - A language model supporting programming tasks, enhancing developer productivity.

## Competitors and Alternatives

Stability AI competes with OpenAI (DALL-E), Google (Imagen, Gemini), Midjourney, Adobe Firefly, and others. Stability AI's differentiation centers on **accessibility and transparency**. While competitors use closed API formats, Stability AI publishes model weights for both academic and commercial use, enabling local execution and fine-tuning.

## Business Strategy and Impact

Stability AI operates under the philosophy that "AI technology should not be monopolized by a few companies," advancing open-source development. This strategy democratizes access to cutting-edge AI for startups, academic institutions, and enterprises without substantial licensing fees. The resulting acceleration in generative AI ecosystem innovation and industry-wide competition has intensified.

## Implementation Best Practices

When running Stable Diffusion locally, **8GB+ VRAM GPUs are recommended**. Cloud environments (Google Colab, Hugging Face) provide alternatives. Prompt engineering, LoRA fine-tuning, and negative prompt optimization are key to high-quality outputs.
