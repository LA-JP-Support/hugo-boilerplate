## What is Shoppable Posts?

**Shoppable Posts are Instagram, Facebook, TikTok posts with product tags, letting you buy without leaving the app.** Before, seeing something great on SNS meant finding the brand's site and buying elsewhere. Shoppable Posts complete the purchase in seconds.

> **In a nutshell:** See a shirt on Instagram and buy it right there.

**Key points:**

- **What it does:** Buy directly from SNS posts using embedded tags
- **Why you need it:** Immediate action when interest peaks on SNS
- **Who uses it:** Fashion, cosmetics, lifestyle brands with SNS followers

## Why it matters

SNS is now the main product discovery channel. Especially Instagram and TikTok—users discover products while scrolling. That "found it! I want it!" moment is crucial. If they can't buy immediately, the opportunity vanishes. Shoppable Posts capture that moment and convert interest directly to sales.

For brands, SNS becomes a pure recognition/engagement tool to an actual sales channel.

## How it works

Implementation is simple. Brand links product catalog to Instagram Business Account. At posting, select product tags and place them on the image. Followers tap tags to see product details with checkout. Instagram handles this natively—no complex setup needed.

You can tag multiple items—"these 4 outfits," for example. It's straightforward commerce.

## Real-world use cases

**Fashion influencers**
Tag outfits. Followers buy directly. Influencers get commission revenue.

**Makeup artists**
Tag every product in makeup videos. Viewers buy immediately.

**Retail chains**
Tag seasonal outfit bundles for coordinated multi-item sales.

**New product launches**
Launch alongside shoppable posts for day-one sales records.

## Benefits and considerations

**Benefits:** Instant-to-checkout (3 clicks max) eliminates cart abandonment. Real-time data shows which post/product sells. Content itself becomes a sales channel, cutting separate marketing costs.

**Considerations:** Inventory sync across platforms prevents overselling. Platform dependency is high—algorithm changes impact reach.

## Frequently asked questions

**Q: Which SNS support shoppable posts?**
A: Instagram, Facebook, TikTok, Pinterest, Snapchat. Features vary by platform.

**Q: What if products sell out?**
A: Most platforms show "sold out" when inventory depletes. Pre-sync is critical.

**Q: What's the success rate?**
A: Regular posts see 30-50% higher conversion rates on average.
