## What is WhatsApp Business?

**WhatsApp Business is a specialized version of WhatsApp enabling companies to communicate with customers.** While personal WhatsApp connects friends, WhatsApp Business allows companies to send customer support, order confirmations, and marketing messages. It includes business features like automated replies, customer management, and message analytics.

> **In a nutshell:** It's like LINE Official Accounts (where businesses have official accounts to contact customers) but for WhatsApp.

**Key points:**

- **What it does:** A platform letting companies send messages, notifications, and support to customers via WhatsApp
- **Why it matters:** Customers already use WhatsApp daily; message open rates exceed 90% compared to email's 20-30%
- **Who uses it:** Restaurants, retailers, banks, healthcare providers, airlines—any business needing customer communication

## Why it matters

Traditionally, customer notifications came via email, SMS, or phone. However, most customers check WhatsApp daily. Message open rates hit 90%+ compared to email's 20-30%. Using WhatsApp Business makes messages nearly impossible to miss.

Combined with chatbots, WhatsApp Business enables 24/7 automated support. When customers inquire about delivery at midnight, automated responses appear immediately. Complex issues escalate automatically to human operators.

WhatsApp Business's catalog feature lets customers browse products within WhatsApp and order directly—no need to visit websites. This eliminates friction and increases conversion rates.

## Key features and services

WhatsApp Business offers two levels: "WhatsApp Business App" for small businesses and "WhatsApp Business API" for enterprises.

The app version provides business profiles (displaying hours, address, website links), automated replies (sending automatic messages when unavailable), quick replies (templates for frequent questions), and message analytics (tracking when customers message).

The API version integrates with company CRM systems, enabling automated personalized messages from customer databases. It automates delivery confirmations, shipping updates, and surveys at scale. Using webhooks, messages trigger automatically from other systems.

## Competitors and alternatives

WhatsApp Business competition varies by region and industry. Japan has LINE Official Account; China has WeChat Store; chatbot competitors include Intercom and Drift. However, WhatsApp dominates internationally, especially in emerging markets.

SMS and email also compete but WhatsApp wins on response rates and immediacy. SMS excels at guaranteed delivery; email suits complex information. Most companies use multiple channels together.

## Business applications

WhatsApp Business serves customer support, shipping updates, appointment confirmations, promotions, payment confirmations, and surveys. In emerging markets like India and Brazil, small stores use WhatsApp Business to handle orders, payments, and deliveries entirely within the app.

## Frequently asked questions

**Q: Is WhatsApp Business really business-only? Can individuals use it?**
A: WhatsApp Business app is available to individuals, but it's designed for business use. The API version requires business registration.

**Q: Can I send marketing messages to customers via WhatsApp Business?**
A: Yes, with prior consent. WhatsApp terms recommend using the API version for marketing, which may require advance approval.

**Q: Are there translation or multi-language features?**
A: WhatsApp Business doesn't include built-in translation, but integration with translation APIs (like Google Translate) enables multi-language support.
