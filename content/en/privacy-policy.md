## Privacy Policy

Interwork Corporation (hereinafter "the Company") is committed to protecting and appropriately handling the personal information of customers in the website construction and operational support service **SmartWeb** that the Company provides. This policy consolidates the Company's personal information protection policy and matters related to the handling of personal information.

---

## 1. Definition of Personal Information

Personal information refers to information related to living individuals that can identify a specific individual, including name, email address, company name, position, inquiry content, and usage history.

---

## 2. Methods and Purposes of Collecting Personal Information

The Company collects personal information through lawful and fair means for the following purposes:

### SmartWeb Service Provision and Operational Management

- Providing functions and support such as ticket management, live chat, support portal, knowledge base, and AI chatbot (support.smartweb.jp)
- Responding to inquiries, troubleshooting, and providing technical support
- Conducting internal analysis and quality improvements for service enhancement and new feature development
- Displaying and notifying urgent announcements and important communications

---

## 3. Purposes for Using Personal Information

Information collected is used within the following scope:

- Setting up SmartWeb services, providing implementation support, and delivering continuous support
- Operating chat and ticket systems, including inquiry response
- Preventing defects and considering improvement measures based on inquiry content and usage patterns

---

## 4. Third-Party Sharing and Joint Use of Personal Information

The Company shares personal information with joint users only in the following cases:

**Joint User:**
Quality Unit s.r.o. (Developer of LiveAgent and FlowHunt, located in Bratislava, Slovakia)

**Items to be Jointly Used:**
Name, company name, position, email address, inquiry content, usage history, and similar information

**Purposes for Use:**
Inquiry response, technical support, service operation, and improvement support

**Responsibility Manager:**
Interwork Corporation

---

## 5. Cooperation with External Services

SmartWeb functions through cooperation with the following external services:

### LiveAgent

Help desk software that provides ticket management, live chat support, support portal, and call center functions

### FlowHunt

A tool that provides AI chatbot and knowledge base functions. Examples of AI chatbot implementation can be verified on the BuildVu product page and support portal.

While inquiry content and chat history are processed and stored on these external tools, the Company takes full care regarding privacy and security, managing them within the minimum necessary scope. For details, please refer to the privacy policies of each external service provider.

---

## 6. Security Measures

The Company implements technical and organizational measures including access control, encryption, employee training, and internal audits to prevent unauthorized disclosure, loss, or damage of personal information, working to strengthen information security.

---

## 7. Response to Disclosure, Correction, and Deletion Requests

Requests from customers regarding disclosure, correction, deletion, or suspension of use of personal information will be appropriately handled in accordance with applicable laws after verifying the identity of the individual.

---

## 8. Use of Cookies and Similar Technologies

SmartWeb may use cookies and similar technologies for service improvement and access analysis; however, the Company does not collect information that can identify individuals.

---

## 9. Changes to Privacy Policy

The Company may revise this policy without notice due to changes in laws or service content. The latest version is posted on the Company's website, so please review it periodically.
