## Overview of Multi-Language Support Feature
### What is Multi-Language Support Feature
**Multi-language support feature** is a function that allows companies expanding globally or those providing customer service in multiple languages to enable their customer support agents (staff) to use LiveAgent in their preferred language.

LiveAgent currently supports **43 languages** and new translations are added regularly.

## Range of Supported Languages
### Diversity of Supported Languages
LiveAgent supports 43 languages worldwide, covering a wide range from major international languages to region-specific languages. This allows companies operating in diverse markets around the world to meet their needs.

## Key Features of Multi-Language Support
### Login Language Settings
Agents can log in and operate LiveAgent in their preferred language. This enables:

- **Intuitive operation**: Working in native language improves work efficiency
- **Reduced learning costs**: Minimizes language barriers during new employee training
- **Stress reduction**: Reduces psychological burden through working in familiar language

### Language-Adaptive Widgets
When customizing and creating widgets in LiveAgent, you can easily set preferred languages for specific departments.

**Usage examples:**

- When expanding business in markets with different languages, website widgets can also be adapted
- Japanese widgets for the Japanese market, English widgets for English-speaking regions
- Customization possible to match region-specific cultural nuances
