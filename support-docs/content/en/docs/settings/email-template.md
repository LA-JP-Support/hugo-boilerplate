## What are Email Templates

A feature that allows you to create templates for the design and content of emails sent to customers and agents. Unifies brand image and enables efficient operation.

### Benefits of Email Templates

For example, by creating templates for frequently sent emails such as inquiry confirmation emails or resolution notifications, you can provide prompt and consistent customer support.

### Main Benefits

**Key Points:**

- Unify email content and design
- Template frequently sent emails
- Maintain brand image
- Improve work efficiency
- Provide consistent support that gives customers peace of mind
