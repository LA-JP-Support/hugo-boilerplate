## External Service Integration Overview

### Number of Supported Services
SmartWeb enables integration with over 200 external services. It features high scalability that can flexibly accommodate diverse corporate workflows and business needs.

### Examples of Major Supported Services
We support major services frequently used in business such as Salesforce, Slack, Google Analytics, Shopify, and WordPress. We cover a wide range of business domains including CRM, communication, analytics, e-commerce, and content management.

## Integration Benefits

### Improved Business Efficiency
Through integration with external services, you can automate tasks that were previously done manually and significantly streamline business processes. It's possible to reduce the hassle of data re-entry and manual transfers while increasing productivity.

### Insights Through Data Integration
By seamlessly integrating data between different services, deeper analysis and decision-making become possible. You can understand business from a comprehensive perspective including customer behavior, sales trends, and business performance.

### Seamless Work Environment
We break down the barriers between each service and realize an integrated workspace. Through centralized information management and smooth information sharing, we improve team productivity and collaboration.

## Integration Methods

### API Integration
We realize flexible and secure integration using RESTful APIs. We provide comprehensive documentation for developers and easy-to-implement SDKs.

### Plugins
We provide easily installable plugins that support many major services. Even administrators without technical knowledge can complete integration with just a few clicks.

### Direct Integration
There are also many services that can be directly integrated on SmartWeb without going through third-party tools. We realize simplified configuration and more direct integration.
