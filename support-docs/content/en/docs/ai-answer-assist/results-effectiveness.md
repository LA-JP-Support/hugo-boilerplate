## Results from Implementing Companies

Companies that have implemented AI Answer Assist (AI Answer Improver) have reported significant business improvement effects.

## Case Study: CVFR Travel Group (Travel Industry)

### Implementation Effects

With the implementation of AI Answer Assist, **overall team productivity improved by 30%**.

### Specific Improvements

- Reduced response time for repetitive inquiries
- Staff can now focus on complex cases and high-value-added tasks
- Achieved uniformity in response quality

## Expected Effects

### Shortened Response Time

- Significantly reduced time spent on document creation
- Streamlined information integration from multiple resources
- Shortened translation work time

### Quality Improvement

- Unified professional writing expression
- Improved document consistency
- Easy addition of technical term explanations

### Reduced Staff Burden

- Reduced stress from document creation
- Automation of repetitive tasks
- Secured time for more creative work

## Scenarios Where AI Answer Assist is Particularly Effective

### Handling Large Volumes of Inquiries

You can quickly create high-quality responses to repetitive inquiries.

### Handling Multiple Products and Services

You can efficiently create responses containing accurate information for inquiries about various products and services.

### Multilingual Support

By utilizing translation functions, you can provide high-quality support while reducing the burden of foreign language correspondence.

## Combined Effects with AI Email Response Creation

By using AI Answer Assist together with AI Email Response Creation (AI Answer Composer), further efficiency improvements are possible. By consistently handling everything from automatic response generation to document refinement, you can simultaneously achieve significant reduction in response time and quality improvement.
