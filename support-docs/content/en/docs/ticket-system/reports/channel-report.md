## What is Channel Report

The Channel Report is a feature that analyzes inquiry status across multiple channels such as email, chat, phone, and social media. It helps identify which channels are most utilized and assists in formulating support strategies.

### Customer Segment Trends in Channel Selection

For example, younger customers tend to prefer chat and social media, while business customers tend to choose email and phone. By referencing channel-specific data, you can prepare appropriate response methods for each segment.

## Benefits of Using Channel Report

It becomes possible to adjust resource allocation for each channel and achieve efficient operations.

### Main Benefits

- Visualize inquiry status by channel
- Understand usage trends by customer segment
- Utilize for strategic resource allocation
- Improve efficiency of multi-channel support
- Enhance the precision of support strategies
