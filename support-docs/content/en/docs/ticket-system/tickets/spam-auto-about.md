## Spam Detection Mechanism

### Spam Filter Learning Mechanism

The ticket spam detection mechanism is a learning-type spam filter that improves accuracy by repeatedly having agents manually mark inappropriate tickets as "spam".

### How to Mark Tickets as Spam

To mark a ticket as spam, agents click the "Spam" button at the bottom of the individual ticket screen.

## Improving Filtering Accuracy

### Initial Stage Filtering

The filtering strength may not be very high when LiveAgent is first implemented.

### Benefits of Continuous Operation

However, through continuous operation, it develops powerful filtering capabilities that contribute to agents' email processing tasks.
