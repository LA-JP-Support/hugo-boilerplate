## Notes Feature Overview

A feature that allows you to add internal memos to tickets. These are not visible to customers and are useful for handovers between staff members and sharing important notes.

### Use Cases

For example, by leaving supplementary information such as "This customer has received a refund in the past," the next staff member can respond with an understanding of the background.

## Main Features

### Adding Private Memos
- Ability to add internal private memos

### Communication Support
- Effective for handovers and sharing important notes
- Enables consistent responses across the entire team

### Information Management
- Manage information without showing unnecessary details to customers
- Improve accuracy of response history
