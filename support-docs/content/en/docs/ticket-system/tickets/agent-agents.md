## What are Agents

In LiveAgent, "Agents" refer to staff members who handle customer inquiries.

### Role of Agents
They are responsible for processing inquiries that come through multiple channels such as email, chat, phone, and social media.

## Features of Agent Management

### Flexible Addition and Removal
For example, you can start with a small team and invite new agents one after another as your business expands. Since unlimited additions are possible, it can flexibly accommodate everything from startups to large enterprises.

### Key Points of Agent Management

#### Channel Support
- Can handle all channels including email, chat, phone, and social media

#### Scalability
- Flexibility to add unlimited agents (costs are incurred per agent)

#### Permission Management
- Can separate permissions such as Admin and Agent roles

#### Individual Management Functions
- Individual management of notification settings, department assignments, profile editing, etc. is possible

#### Team Expansion
- Can quickly strengthen your structure in line with team expansion
