## Overview of Ticket Merge

Ticket merge is a feature that consolidates multiple tickets related to the same customer or identical content into one. It enables centralized management even when inquiries are scattered.

### Specific Examples of Ticket Merge

For example, when the same customer sends the same problem via both email and chat, tickets can be merged and consolidated into a single thread. This prevents duplicate responses and confusion.

## Benefits of Ticket Merge

### Main Advantages

- Consolidate duplicate tickets into one
- Organize customer inquiry history
- Prevent duplicate responses for improved efficiency
- Achieve consistent support
- Contribute to improved customer satisfaction
