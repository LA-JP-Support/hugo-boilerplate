## POP3 Access Setup Guide for Google Apps Email Account

### Overview
To integrate your Google Apps-hosted email account with LiveAgent, follow these steps.

### Step 1: Set Access Permissions in Google Apps Account
Log in to your Google Apps account and grant access to your email account.

For detailed instructions, please refer to:
https://support.google.com/a/answer/105694?hl=ja

### Step 2: Enable POP3 Access in Gmail Account
1. Log in to the Gmail email account you want to connect to LiveAgent.
2. Configure settings from "Settings" > "Forwarding and POP/IMAP".

#### Important Note
Please wait approximately 30 minutes for Google to replicate these settings.

### Step 3: Add Email Account in LiveAgent
1. Log in to your LiveAgent administrator account.
2. Select "Configuration" > "Email" > "Mail accounts" > "Add mail account".

### Post-Integration Behavior
All incoming emails to your Google Apps email account will now be converted into tickets and stored in LiveAgent.
