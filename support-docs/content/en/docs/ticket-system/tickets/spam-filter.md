## Overview of Spam Filters

Spam filters are a feature that automatically filters out spam emails and unwanted inquiries. Support staff can focus on handling legitimate customer support.

### Main Benefits of Spam Filters

Reduces the risk of mistakenly processing spam as customer inquiries and eliminates wasted effort. Accurate classification also improves response efficiency.

## Feature Details

### Spam Detection Characteristics

**Key Points:**

- Automatically detect spam emails
- Exclude unwanted inquiries from the system
- Reduce agent workload
- Enable focus on legitimate customer support
- Significantly improve response efficiency

## Expected Results

### Improved Operational Efficiency

The implementation of spam filters can be expected to deliver the following benefits:
- Improved support team productivity
- Enhanced quality of customer support
- Reduced unnecessary work time
