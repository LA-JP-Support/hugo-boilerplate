## Overview of Online Ticket History URL

### Feature Description
A feature that allows you to share the complete history of a ticket as a URL. You can easily present the response history to customers or other departments.

### Usage Scenarios
For example, when a customer says "I want to check our previous interactions," you can send them a dedicated URL to review the past history at a glance. This is also useful for inter-departmental coordination.

## Key Features

### Streamlined Information Sharing
- Share ticket history in URL format
- Facilitate smooth information sharing with customers and other departments

### Response Tracking
- Easily review response history
- Useful for inter-departmental coordination and auditing

### Customer Experience
- Enhance customer confidence
