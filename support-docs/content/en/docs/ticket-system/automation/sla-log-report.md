## Overview of SLA Log Report

The SLA Log Report is a feature that records and tracks all history related to SLAs. You can view in detail when, on which tickets, and how SLAs were complied with or violated.

## Main Purpose of SLA Log Report

### SLA History Management
Administrators can use this report to identify trends and causes of SLA violations. For example, it's possible to discover issues concentrated in specific time periods or departments.

### Utilization for Business Improvement
This promotes the implementation of recurrence prevention measures and improvements to business processes.

## Key Feature Points

### Detailed History Tracking
- Records and tracks SLA history in detail
- Clarifies compliance and violation status

### Analysis and Improvement
- Enables identification of violation trends and causes
- Useful for formulating recurrence prevention measures
- Serves as a foundation for promoting business improvement
