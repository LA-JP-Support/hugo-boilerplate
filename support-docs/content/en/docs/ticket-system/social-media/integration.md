## Social Media Advertising Integration Overview

Yes, integration with social media advertising is possible.

### Supported Advertising Platforms

- Comments on Facebook/Instagram ad posts
- Messenger inquiries via advertisements

## Key Features of Integration

### Automatic Ticket Management
- **Automatic Ticket Creation**: Automatically converts ad responses into tickets
- **Rapid Response**: Centralized management of replies to ad comments
- **Performance Measurement**: Track inquiry volume by advertisement

## Use Cases

### Specific Operational Benefits
- Dedicated team handling campaign ad inquiries
- Early detection and response to ad comment controversies
- Measuring inquiry volume as an advertising effectiveness KPI

SNS integration functionality is an optional feature. Please contact us for more details.
