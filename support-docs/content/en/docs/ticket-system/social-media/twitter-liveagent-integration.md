## Twitter and LiveAgent Integration Steps

### Introduction
**We will guide you through the steps to set up integration between Twitter and LiveAgent.**

### Integration Steps

#### Log in to LiveAgent
**1. Log in to LiveAgent.**

#### Add Twitter Account
**2. Navigate to "Settings" - "Twitter" - "Twitter Accounts" and click "Add Twitter Account".**

![](/liveagent/scripts/file.php?view=Y&file=4c5dc71168c314d06ea672a0d52be835)

#### Authenticate Twitter Account
**3. On the displayed screen, log in to your Twitter account and click "Authorize app".**

![](/liveagent/scripts/file.php?view=Y&file=787a8d2127b8e65cb1b7fe12b25f45a8)

#### Integration Complete
**4. Your Twitter account has been successfully integrated.**

![](/liveagent/scripts/file.php?view=Y&file=e803bee7fcb25efbab8b510606767aa4)

#### Edit Account Settings
**5. Click "Edit" on the above screen to enable account integration and select the "Team" to assign tickets to.**

![](/liveagent/scripts/file.php?view=Y&file=6ec14706492132a605a6bbe7bd0ca8f9)
