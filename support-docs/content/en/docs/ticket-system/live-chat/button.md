## What is a Chat Button
An entry point to chat functionality that can be placed on websites. Position it prominently at page edges or as floating display to guide users to real-time support or offline messaging when clicked. Customize colors, shapes, text, and positioning to match your brand while providing intuitive "contact us" pathways for first-time visitors.

## Benefits and Effects
After implementation, you can consolidate initial inquiries through chat, reducing back-and-forth communication compared to email or phone, leading to shorter resolution times. Optimizing design and messaging through A/B testing can also improve chat initiation rates.

## Key Features

### Customizability
- Flexible customization of appearance (colors, shapes, text) and display position

### Response Modes
- Switch behavior between online/offline modes

### Multi-language Support
- Designed to easily support multilingual websites

### Improved Chat Initiation Rates
- Enhance visibility techniques to boost chat initiation rates

### Support Efficiency
- Streamline support channels to avoid missing inquiries
