## Complete Overview of the AI Chatbot Plan

### Achieving Ultimate Customer Support 24/7/365

SmartWeb's AI Chatbot Plan is an innovative solution that leverages the latest AI platform "FlowHunt" and LiveAgent's chat system to automatically answer frequently asked questions even outside business hours and holidays. Complex questions that AI cannot handle are smoothly transferred to human staff through the "escalation function," allowing small and medium-sized businesses to build comprehensive customer support systems comparable to large enterprises at low cost without inconveniencing customers.

## 1. Revolutionary Support System Operating 24/7/365

### Non-Stop Customer Service

**Fully Automated 24-Hour Response** Whether it's 2 AM, New Year holidays, or Obon vacation, the AI chatbot continues customer service without rest. It can normally be used casually as anonymous chat, and by registering an email address in advance, continuous support linked to customer history is also possible.

### Detailed Amazing Response Capabilities

- Can simultaneously converse with 100 customers
- Instant response with zero wait time
- Always polite responses without becoming emotional
- All AI conversations are automatically converted to tickets for complete visibility of response status

## 2. Accurate and Reliable Answers Through Latest Technology

### Innovation of RAG Technology

FlowHunt adopts the latest mechanism called **RAG (Retrieval-Augmented Generation) technology**.

#### Detailed Explanation of RAG Technology

**What is RAG Technology?** An abbreviation for "Retrieval-Augmented Generation," this technology first searches for accurate information from your company's documents before the AI responds, then creates answers based on that information.

### Advanced Information Management Mechanisms

#### Knowledge Sources Function

**What are Knowledge Sources?** A function that manages the "information sources" for AI learning. Your company can freely select and configure information sources such as:

- Your company's website (automatically retrieves latest information periodically)
- Product catalogs and brochures (PDF)
- Frequently asked questions (FAQ)
- Internal manuals and technical documents
- Price lists and specification sheets

## 3. AI Reliability and Accuracy Improvement

### Significant Reduction in Incorrect Answers

According to latest research results, AI chatbots using RAG technology achieve:

- 42-68% reduction in hallucinations (AI incorrect answers)
- Up to 89% accuracy in specific fields
