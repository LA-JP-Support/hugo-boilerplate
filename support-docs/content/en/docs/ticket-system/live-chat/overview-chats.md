## Overview of Chats Overview

Visualizing operational status in chat management is an important strategy for maintaining an efficient customer support system.

### Operational Status Visualization

Get a bird's-eye view of ongoing and completed chat situations, understanding congestion levels, response times, and agent workload. Visualizing operational status supports on-site decision making.

### Real-time Monitoring

Monitor queues and average response times on the dashboard, making decisions about member reallocation or escalation as needed. Prevent SLA violations before they occur.

## Key Features and Benefits

### Visualization Points

- Visualize queues, response times, and agent workload
- Real-time understanding of operational status through dashboard
- Utilize for immediate review of staffing and priorities
- Early detection of signs of SLA violations
- Provide metrics that serve as starting points for improvement cycles
