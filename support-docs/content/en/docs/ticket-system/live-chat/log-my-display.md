## How to Disable Automatic Chat Log Sending

### Default State
By default, the customer-side chat window displays a checkbox labeled "Send chat log to myself". This setting may be unnecessary or bothersome for many users, creating a need to hide or disable it.

### Steps to Disable Chat Log Sending

1. Go to "Settings"
2. Select the "Email" section
3. Open "Customer Email Templates"
4. In "Chat Conversation History", click "Disable this email"

#### Benefits of Disabling
Disabling automatic chat log sending provides the following benefits:

- Reduction of unnecessary emails
- Privacy protection
- Email inbox organization

#### Important Considerations
When disabling chat log sending, you may need to save important conversation records through alternative methods. For example:

- Saving screenshots
- Recording in internal sharing systems
- Manual log preservation

#### Recommended Operational Methods
The optimal chat log management method varies depending on the company and usage scenarios. Consider the following points:

- Nature of customer support
- Information security policies
- Team communication methods

By adjusting settings this way, you can achieve a more efficient and comfortable chat support experience.
