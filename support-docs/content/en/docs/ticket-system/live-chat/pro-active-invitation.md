## Overview of Proactive Chat Invitations

Proactive chat invitations allow you to proactively display chat invitations to visitors who meet specific conditions. This enables timely intervention for pre-departure follow-ups and high-value product purchase support.

By fine-tuning the invitation messaging, timing, and target conditions, you can maximize effectiveness while minimizing the intrusive popup experience.

## Key Features and Benefits

### Proactive Customer Approach
- Enable proactive outreach based on specific conditions
- Effective for preventing abandonment and supporting high-value product purchases

### Optimization Strategy
### Enhanced Communication Quality
- Improve effectiveness through optimized messaging and display timing
- Avoid excessive displays that could harm user experience

### Maximized Results
- Boost outcomes for both sales and support functions
