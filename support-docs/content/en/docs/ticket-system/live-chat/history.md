## Chat History Overview

Chat history in customer support is an essential tool that significantly improves the quality and efficiency of communication.

### Key Features and Benefits

Search and reference past chat conversations to provide contextually informed responses. Quickly understand customer background even when handling staff changes or consultations spanning across time periods.

### Integration with Customer Profiles

Since history is linked to customer profiles, you can immediately check previous agreements and pending matters. This reduces the burden of re-explanation and ensures continuity in customer experience.

## Key Features

### Efficient Information Management
- Cross-search past conversations to share context
- Centrally manage customer profiles and history

### Improved Support Quality
- Streamline handovers during staff transitions
- Reduce re-explanation burden and improve satisfaction

### Additional Applications
- Can also be utilized for quality audits and training purposes
