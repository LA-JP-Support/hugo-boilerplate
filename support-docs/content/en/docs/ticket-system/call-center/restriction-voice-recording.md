## Overview of Unlimited Call Recording

LiveAgent's call recording feature provides a system that can store all calls without limits. This is an essential function for improving support quality, educational purposes, and compliance requirements.

## Main Benefits of Call Recording

### Agent Training and Quality Improvement
Recording data can be used for training purposes, allowing agents to review past conversations and identify areas for improvement.

### Troubleshooting and Evidence Preservation
In case of disputes with customers, recordings can be presented as evidence.

## Benefits of Unlimited Recording

### Flexibility and Scalability
With unlimited recording capabilities, businesses of any size can use the system with confidence.

### Long-term Customer Relationship Building
The ability to store all past interactions contributes to building long-term customer relationships.

## Key Feature Points

- Record and store all calls without limits
- Available for agent training and quality improvement
- Effective for fact verification and evidence presentation during disputes
- Compliant with compliance requirements
- Supports long-term customer relationship maintenance
