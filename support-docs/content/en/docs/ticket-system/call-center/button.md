## What is a Call Button

A call button that can be installed on websites, allowing customers to directly call support with one click. It bridges the gap with online visitors and enables instant communication.

### Benefits and Use Cases

For example, when installed on an e-commerce product page, customers can immediately ask operators questions while considering a purchase. Since responses are faster than inquiry forms or emails, this directly leads to improved conversion rates.

### Security and Convenience

The support team doesn't need to disclose phone numbers, and customers can make calls directly from their browser, which provides peace of mind from a security perspective.

## Key Features

### Enhanced Customer Experience

- Customers can make calls with one click
- Faster response than forms or emails
- Prompt support for customers in the purchase consideration stage

### Security and Business Benefits

- Safe usage without disclosing phone numbers
- Direct impact on conversion rate improvement
