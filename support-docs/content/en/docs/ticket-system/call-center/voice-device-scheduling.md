## Overview of Call Device Scheduling

A feature that allows scheduling of devices used for calls. It enables flexible switching based on business hours and staff availability.

### Usage Examples

For example, you can set up configurations such as using an office desk phone in the morning and a home softphone in the afternoon. This makes it easier to accommodate remote work and shift work arrangements.

## Benefits for Companies

For companies with multiple locations or remote work arrangements, the ability to build a stable customer service system is a significant advantage.

## Key Features

### Device Switching
- Automatic switching of devices by time period
- Support for flexible work styles

### Business Response Flexibility
- Compatible with remote work and shift work
- Consistently ensures call quality
- Guarantees continuity of customer service
