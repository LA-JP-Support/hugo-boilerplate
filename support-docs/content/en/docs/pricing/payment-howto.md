## Payment Overview
We will explain the initial fees and costs at the start of your subscription.

### Initial Fees
At the time of contract, we will charge initial fees and 3 months' worth of estimated monthly fees.

### Costs During Implementation Period
Implementation work will begin after payment confirmation. Please note that costs will accrue from the start of service, including the implementation preparation period.

## Payment Methods
From the 4th month after implementation, you can choose from the following payment methods:

### Monthly Payment
- Credit card payment

### Annual Lump Sum Payment
- Invoice payment
- Credit card payment

## Usage-Based Charges
When usage-based charges occur, we will bill you accordingly each time.
