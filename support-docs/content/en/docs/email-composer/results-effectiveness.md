## Results from Implementing Companies

Companies that have implemented AI email response creation (AI Answer Composer) report significant operational improvement effects.

## Case Study 1: CS Apparel Group (Apparel Company)

### 75% Reduction in Email Response Time

By implementing the AI response creation feature, they **reduced average email response time from 24 hours to 6 hours, achieving a 75% reduction**.

### Specific Improvements

- Staff no longer need to write responses from scratch
- Achieved significant time reduction while maintaining response quality
- Increased number of cases handled per day

## Case Study 2: CVFR Travel Group (Travel Industry)

- Reduced response time for repetitive inquiries
