## Integration with AI Response Assistant

Response drafts generated by AI Email Response Creation (AI Answer Composer) are **seamlessly transferred to the AI Response Assistant (AI Answer Improver) screen**.

## Integration Flow

### Step 1: Automatic Response Draft Generation

AI Email Response Creation automatically generates response drafts from conversation history and knowledge base.

### Step 2: Transfer to AI Response Assistant

The generated response draft is transferred directly to the AI Response Assistant editing screen.

### Step 3: Refinement

Use AI Response Assistant functions to further optimize the response draft.

**Available functions:**
- Improve: Make text clearer and more professional
- Extend: Enhance detailed explanations and additional information
- Simplify: Summarize complex content concisely
- Custom instructions: Translation, adding explanations for technical terms, etc.

### Step 4: Use in Reply

When satisfied, use the "Use in Response" button to insert into ticket reply, then send after final confirmation.

## Benefits of Integration

### No Need to Start from Scratch

AI Email Response Creation creates the foundation, and AI Response Assistant refines it, eliminating the need for agents to write from scratch.

### Significant Reduction in Response Time

The combination of two AI functions achieves significant reduction in response time.

### High-Precision Response Creation

With accurate information from the knowledge base + text optimization, you can efficiently create high-precision responses that resonate with customers.

## Use Cases

### Handling Complex Technical Inquiries

1. **AI Email Response Creation** generates response draft from technical documents and FAQs
2. **AI Response Assistant** is instructed to "add explanations for technical terms"
3. A technically accurate and easy-to-understand response is completed

### Handling Foreign Language Inquiries

1. **AI Email Response Creation** generates response draft in Japanese
2. **AI Response Assistant** is instructed to "translate to English"
3. A naturally expressed foreign language response is completed
