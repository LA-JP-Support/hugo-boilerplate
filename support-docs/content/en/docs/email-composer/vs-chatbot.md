## Differences Between AI Email Response Generation and AI Chatbots

SmartWeb provides two AI features: AI Email Response Generation (AI Answer Composer) and AI Chatbots. This article explains the characteristics and use cases for each.

## Comparison Table

| Item | AI Email Response Generation | AI Chatbot |
|------|------------------------------|------------|
| Supported Channels | Email・Inquiry Forms | Website Chat |
| Output Method | Displays draft for staff | Responds directly to customers |
| Review Process | Staff reviews before sending | Automatic immediate response |
| Final Responsibility | Staff member | AI (with escalation option) |
| Response Speed | After staff review | Real-time |
| Operating Hours | Staff working hours | 24/7/365 |

## Features of AI Email Response Generation

### Staff-Led Response

AI creates drafts, and staff members review and edit before sending. Staff members have final control over response content.

### Suitable Scenarios

- Complex inquiries
- Content requiring high accuracy
- Complaint and grievance handling
- Inquiries involving contracts or legal content
- Cases requiring individual attention

## Features of AI Chatbots

### Fully Automated Response

AI responds to customer questions 24/7/365 in real-time. Response is completed without staff intervention.

### Suitable Scenarios

- Frequently asked questions (FAQ)
- Product information inquiries
- After-hours support
- Multilingual support
- Handling large volumes of inquiries

## Key Points for Use Case Selection

### When Immediacy is Important → AI Chatbot

For real-time questions from website visitors, AI chatbots are most suitable.

### When Accuracy and Caution are Important → AI Email Response Generation

When you want staff to review content before sending, AI email response generation is most suitable.

### Combining Both

Many companies use both AI features together.

**Example:**
- Website inquiries → AI chatbot responds
- Email inquiries → AI email response generation creates draft, staff reviews and sends
- When AI chatbot cannot resolve → Escalate and handle with AI email response generation
