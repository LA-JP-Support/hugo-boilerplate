## What is FlowHunt

FlowHunt is a **no-code flow design tool** that enables building AI chatbots and automated response systems.

### Key Features

You can design conversation and processing flows through drag-and-drop without programming, and **realize processes that traditionally required individual specialized knowledge, such as image analysis, PDF processing, and natural language responses, within a single flow**.

### AI Models and Information Integration

You can freely select and utilize multiple large language models (LLMs) such as ChatGPT and Claude, and combine knowledge sources like websites, PDFs, and videos to generate accurate, contextually appropriate responses.

### Application Areas and Functionality

FlowHunt is utilized across **a wide range of fields including customer support, marketing, and internal business automation**, starting with SmartWeb. In addition to AI-powered automated responses, it also supports **escalation to human chat** and **automatic form submission for inquiries**.

It realizes flexible AI operations tailored to each company's requirements.
