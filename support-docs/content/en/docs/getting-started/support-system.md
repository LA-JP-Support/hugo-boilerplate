## Three-Tier Support System

SmartWeb provides a **comprehensive three-tier support system**.

| Tier | Provider | Language | Content |
|----|------|----------|------|
| **Tier 1** | Interwork (SmartWeb) | Japanese | Primary contact & general support |
| **Tier 2** | LiveAgent | English (24/7) | Advanced technical issues |
| **Tier 3** | FlowHunt | English | AI functionality specialized support |

## Japanese Support (Interwork)

**Customers can basically complete everything with Japanese support only**.

### Support Content

| Content | Description |
|------|------|
| **Primary Contact** | Japanese staff response |
| **Proxy Support** | Acting as intermediary for LiveAgent and FlowHunt inquiries |
| **Post-Implementation Tuning** | Free consultation for feature enhancement |
| **Continuous Improvement** | Optimization proposals aligned with business growth |

### Customer Benefits

- Peace of mind without language barriers
- We handle communication with overseas vendors
- Support tailored to Japanese business practices
