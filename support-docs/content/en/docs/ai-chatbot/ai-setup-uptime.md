## Overall AI Chatbot Implementation Process

SmartWeb's AI chatbot implementation proceeds with a phased approach and comprehensive support system:

The content includes tasks for **our company** and the client.

## Phase 1: Preparation & Design (1 week)

### Requirements Definition and Project Planning

- **Requirements Definition Interview**: Clarification of business objectives and coverage scope - Web meeting (our company & client)
- **Knowledge Survey**: Inventory of existing documents and FAQs (client)
- **Scope Setting**: Determination of question categories the chatbot will handle (client)

### Operations Design

- **Escalation Conditions**: Design of handover timing to humans (client)
- **Operations Structure Planning**: Confirmation of responsible parties and operational flows (client)

## Phase 2: Construction & Configuration (1-2 weeks)

### System Environment Construction

- **FlowHunt Environment Construction**: Workspace creation and API configuration (our company)
- **LiveAgent Integration Setup**: Implementation of seamless escalation functionality (our company)

### Knowledge Base Development

- **Knowledge Base Construction**:
  - Website crawling setup (our company)
  - Document upload and classification (our company)
  - Q&A list creation and registration (client)

### AI Agent Adjustment

- **System Configuration**:
  - System message configuration (our company)
  - Response tone and style adjustment (our company)
  - Tool selection and integration setup (our company)

### Design Adjustment

- **Design Customization**: Appearance adjustment to match brand image (our company)

## Phase 3: Testing & Tuning (1 week)

### Functionality Testing

- **Basic Function Verification**:
  - Basic response and escalation operation confirmation (client)
  - Accuracy adjustment and response quality improvement (our company & client)

### Performance Evaluation

- **Technical Verification**:
  - Load testing and response time measurement (client)
  - Usability testing (verification in actual usage scenarios) (client)

### Final Adjustment

- **Final Configuration**: Feedback reflection and configuration optimization (our company)

## Phase 4: Production Launch & Support

### Phased Rollout

- **Publication Process**: Deployment from limited users to full rollout (client)

### Continuous Monitoring

- **Operations Monitoring**:
  - Continuous monitoring of performance and satisfaction (client)
  - Initial operations period support provision (our company)

### Improvement Cycle

- **Regular Reviews**:
  - Effectiveness measurement and improvement point identification (our company & client)
  - Knowledge addition and feature enhancement planning and execution (our company & client)

For post-implementation processes and improvement methods, please refer to "AI Chatbot Post-Implementation Processes and Improvement Methods."
