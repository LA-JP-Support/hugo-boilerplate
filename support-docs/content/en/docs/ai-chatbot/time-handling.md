## Feasibility of 24/7 Support

### 24/7/365 Support with AI Chatbots

SmartWeb's AI chatbots operate continuously 24 hours a day, 365 days a year without interruption. Unlike traditional human customer support, they can provide fast and accurate responses regardless of time zones or holidays.

### Benefits: Always-On Instant Response

Immediate answers are available even during late nights and holidays, minimizing customer wait times. The main advantages include:

- 24/7/365 inquiry availability
- Instant responses for improved customer satisfaction
- Efficient utilization of human resources
- Freedom from geographical and time constraints

### Continuous Learning and Information Updates

AI chatbots automatically and regularly learn from websites and support portals, always providing responses with the latest information. Through machine learning algorithms, the following evolution is possible:

- Continuous learning from accumulated inquiry data
- Improved understanding accuracy through natural language processing
- Real-time information updates
- Response optimization through user interactions
