## AI Chatbot Customization Overview

Yes, various customizations are possible.

## Customizable Features

### Basic Customization Options

- **Appearance**: Chat window colors, design, and logo
- **Response Style**: Formality level, response length, and tone
- **Training Data**: FAQ, manuals, and website content

### Advanced Customization Options

- **API Integration**: Connection with inventory systems and booking systems
- **Custom Flows**: Business workflows such as reservation handling and quote requests
- **Multi-language Support**: Automatic responses in over 100 languages
